COVID-19 Vaccines and tests

On September 9, 2021, The President of the United States announced a protocol to follow to fight the COVID-19 pandemic, including the requirement for companies with more than 100 employees to mandate vaccinations or weekly testing.

We can help you keep compliant with these government directives while delivering the insight to make the best possible decisions. Contact a representative about making a plan that fits your business strategy.

01
COVID-19 PCR test

If you think you may have an active COVID-19 infection, the PCR test is for you. It can be performed at work or home and will provide you with results in two to four days.
On-site: Number one lab will collect the sample and help with any preparation as well as shipment of the sample.
At home: We provide a home-use test kit that can be sent to employees or students. With a simple nasal swab, the sample will be collected and the test will be performed.

02
COVID-19 Antigen Point-of-Care Testing

Antigen testing is for patients who think they have an active infection. The test can be made at work for asymptomatic employees or at school for students, yielding results in just 15 to 20 minutes.

03
COVID-19 IgG Antibody Testing

If you no longer have symptoms but you believe you may have had COVID-19 in the recent past, you can check your system for antibodies that would have developed after being exposed to the virus using an antibody test. Results are usually ready within two to four days.

On-site: This test comes as a dry blood finger test or a blood draw and can be conveniently performed at your location.

Students or employees with an active COVID-19 infection should contact their healthcare provider.
